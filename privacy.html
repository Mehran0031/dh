1. Introduction and Our Commitment

Welcome to Digital Harmony Studio's Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share your information. This policy explains our privacy practices for our AI-powered music generation and distribution platform, including our integrations with social media platforms like Snapchat, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, TikTok, and music streaming services.

Our Privacy Philosophy: We believe that privacy is a fundamental right. We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services, and we give you control over your data. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Our commitment extends beyond mere compliance with privacy regulations. We have designed our platform with privacy-by-design principles, ensuring that data protection is integrated into every aspect of our service architecture. This comprehensive approach means that privacy considerations are embedded in our AI algorithms, data processing workflows, and user interface design from the ground up.

We understand that trust is earned through consistent action and transparency. Therefore, we provide clear explanations of our data practices, offer meaningful choices about your information, and maintain robust security measures to protect your data. Our privacy practices are regularly reviewed and updated to reflect evolving privacy standards and user expectations.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways to provide you with our AI music generation and distribution services. The information we collect falls into carefully defined categories, each serving specific purposes in delivering and improving our services.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you interact with our platform, you provide various types of information that enable us to deliver personalized music generation and distribution services.

Account Information

Your account information forms the foundation of your Digital Harmony Studio experience. This includes your name and email address, which serve as your primary identifiers within our system. We collect your username and password to secure your account access, along with profile information and preferences that help us customize your experience. Contact information enables us to communicate important service updates and respond to your inquiries.

We also collect information about your music creation preferences, including your preferred genres, typical track specifications, and creative requirements. This information is crucial for our AI algorithms to generate music that aligns with your artistic vision and style preferences.

Music Creation Data

The heart of our service revolves around understanding your creative intentions and translating them into high-quality music content. We collect detailed information about your music genre preferences, which helps our AI models understand the stylistic elements you favor. Track specifications and requirements provide technical parameters that guide the generation process, ensuring the output meets your specific needs.

Creative prompts and descriptions you provide are analyzed by our natural language processing systems to understand the emotional tone, narrative elements, and artistic direction you want to achieve. The generated music content itself becomes part of your creative portfolio within our platform, stored securely and accessible according to your preferences.

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

Our platform automatically collects certain information to ensure optimal performance, security, and user experience. This automated collection is designed to be minimally intrusive while providing the necessary data for service improvement.

Usage Analytics: We collect information about how you use our platform, including pages visited, features used, time spent, and interaction patterns. This helps us improve our AI algorithms and user experience.

Device information, including your device type, operating system, and browser details, helps us optimize our platform's performance across different technical environments. IP address and location data, collected only with your consent, enable us to provide region-appropriate services and comply with local regulations.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Integrations

When you choose to connect your accounts from other platforms, we may receive specific information that enhances your Digital Harmony Studio experience while respecting the privacy policies of those platforms.

Snapchat Integration: Through Snapchat's Login Kit and Creative Kit, we may receive your display name, Bitmoji avatar, and sharing permissions. This integration is designed to comply fully with Snapchat's privacy requirements and developer guidelines. We only access the minimum information necessary to provide the integration functionality you've requested.

5. Data Security and Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Our security approach encompasses technical safeguards, administrative controls, and physical security measures.

Security Commitment: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure data transmission, and regular security audits.

5.1 Technical Safeguards

Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption standards. This ensures that your information remains protected whether it's being transmitted between systems or stored in our databases. Encryption keys are managed through secure key management systems with strict access controls.

Access Controls: We implement strict employee access controls with multi-factor authentication requirements for all system access. Access to user data is granted on a need-to-know basis and is regularly reviewed and updated. All access attempts are logged and monitored for unusual activity.

5.2 Data Retention and Deletion

Our data retention practices are designed to balance service functionality with privacy protection, ensuring that we retain information only as long as necessary for legitimate business purposes.

  • Account Information: Retained while your account remains active and for a reasonable period after account closure
  • Generated Music Content: Stored according to your preferences and applicable legal requirements
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated usage analytics data is retained for service improvement purposes
  • Deleted Data: Securely purged from our systems within 30 days of deletion request

7. Snapchat-Specific Privacy Considerations

Our integration with Snapchat is designed to fully comply with Snapchat's privacy requirements and developer guidelines while providing you with enhanced music sharing capabilities.

📱 Snapchat Integration Details

Our integration with Snapchat is designed to comply with Snapchat's privacy requirements and developer guidelines:
  • Login Kit: We only access your display name and Bitmoji avatar with your explicit consent
  • Creative Kit: Content sharing is always initiated by you and subject to your explicit consent
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the minimum data necessary for functionality
  • No Social Graph Recreation: We do not attempt to recreate friend connections

7.1 Snapchat Login Kit Integration

We use Snapchat Login Kit to provide you with a convenient authentication option that leverages your existing Snapchat account. This integration is designed with privacy-first principles and minimal data collection.

Our Login Kit implementation only accesses your display name and Bitmoji avatar with your explicit consent. We do not request or access your Snapchat friends list, private messages, or other sensitive account information. The authentication process is handled securely through Snapchat's official APIs, ensuring that your Snapchat credentials are never directly handled by our systems.

7.2 Snapchat Creative Kit Integration

Creative Kit integration enables you to share your generated music content directly to Snapchat with enhanced creative features. This sharing is always initiated by you and subject to your explicit consent for each piece of content you choose to share.

Content shared through Creative Kit includes only the music content and associated metadata that you specifically select for sharing. We do not store or access content that you share through Snapchat, and all sharing activities are subject to Snapchat's own privacy policy and terms of service.
